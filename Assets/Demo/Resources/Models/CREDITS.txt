Demo Asset Credits
==================

The 3D models in `Assets/Demo/Resources/Models/` are by Kenney
(https://kenney.nl) and are licensed under Creative Commons Zero (CC0).

  License text: https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/

We're shipping them inside the demo so the WebGL preview works out of
the box, but the canonical source is at:

  - kenney.nl/assets/pirate-kit            (barrel, crate, chest, cannon, rocks-a, bottle, colormap)
  - kenney.nl/assets/animated-characters-1 (characterMedium.fbx)
  - kenney.nl/assets/animated-characters-2 (skaterMaleA.png skin)

Crediting Kenney isn't required by the licence, but we appreciate his
work — these kits have been making the indie scene more legible for a
decade.

The mesh-fracture engine itself is independent of these assets. You can
delete `Assets/Demo/` entirely if you only want the runtime
(`Assets/MeshFracture/`).
